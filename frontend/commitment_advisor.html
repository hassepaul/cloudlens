ℹ How it works

CloudLens analyses 90 days of normalised FOCUS records per service and cloud. It fits a Holt-Winters triple exponential smoothing model to measure forecast stability (MAPE), detects trend direction, and identifies weekly calendar peaks. A confidence score (0–1) combines stability, trend risk, and any planned events you have declared. Services with confidence ≥ 0.70 are recommended for immediate commitment; others are flagged to wait until usage stabilises.

Discount rates shown are conservative no-upfront estimates: Azure Savings Plans (35%/52%), AWS Savings Plans (27%/38%/57%), GCP Committed Use (37%/55%).
